Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredients :

    For Rice :

  • Basmati Rice (1 kg)
  • 4 Cloves
  • Cinnamon (1/2 inch)
  • 2 Green Cardamom pods
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee (1/4 cup)

    • To marinate the chicken :

  • Chicken with bone (1 kg)
  • 4 sliced Onions
  • Barista or Fried Onion (2 tablespoons)
  • Saffron water (1 tablespoon)
  • 2 sprigs of mint leaves
  • Curd (1/2 cup)
  • Coriander powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Deggi chilli powder (1 tablespoon)
  • Green chilli Paste (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Ginger-garlic Paste (1 tablespoon)
  • 3-4 green chillies (slit)
  • Salt to taste

    • Other Ingredients :

  • Ghee (1 tablespoon)
  • Water (1/4 cup)
  • Milk (1/2 cup)
  • Saffron water (2 tablespoons)
  • Ghee (1 tablespoon)
  • Few mint leaves
  • Barista or Fried Onion (1 tablespoon)
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron water (2 teaspoons)
  • Rose water (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Drop of Kewra water

Recipe :

    For marinade :

  1. In a mixing bowl, add chicken and marinate with all the ingredients.
  2. Let the chicken marinade preferably overnight or for minimum 3 hours.

    3. For Rice :

  3. Let the rinsed rice rest for 20 minutes.
  4. Heat water in pot, add ghee and salt.
  5. Add in cloves, cinnamon and green cardamom. Add rice and let it come to a boil. Immediately lower the flame and cook on low flame for 80%.

    6. For Biryani :

  6. In a heavy bottom pan, add ghee and marinated chicken. Cook for about 7-8 minutes.
  7. In another pan, layer the biryani. Add rice, chicken and then top it off with rice. Add in the chicken gravy on top.
  8. In the chicken's pan, add water, milk, saffron water, ghee, mint leaves, barista, salt and coriander leaves. Add this jhol in the biryani.
  9. Add some more saffron water, rose water and few drops of kewra water. Now keep it on dum for 15-20 minutes on low flame.
