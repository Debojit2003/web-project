OurRecipes

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a flavorful Indian dish featuring marinated chicken in a creamy tomato sauce, loved for its rich taste.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala is a delectable Indian dish with marinated and grilled chicken served in a tomato-based creamy sauce, beloved for its bold flavors and global popularity.

Chicken Dum Biryani

Chicken dum biryani is a fragrant Indian rice dish cooked with marinated chicken and aromatic spices, layered and slow-cooked to perfection, offering a delightful blend of flavors.

Chana Masala

Chana masala is a flavorful Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a spicy tomato-based gravy, infused with aromatic spices, and loved for its rich taste and texture.

Palak Paneer

Palak paneer is a popular vegetarian Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy, loved for its savory flavors.

Dal Makhani

Dal makhani is a rich and creamy Punjabi lentil dish made with black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans, cooked with butter and aromatic spices, a beloved Indian comfort food.
