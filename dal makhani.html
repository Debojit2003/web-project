Dal Makhani

Ingredients :

  • Black Gram Dal (3/4 cup)
  • Kidney Beans/Rajma (1/4 cup)
  • Water
  • 4 chopped tomatoes
  • Cooking Oil (1 tablespoon)
  • 2 chopped onions
  • Ginger-garlic Paste (1 teaspoon)
  • Paprika or Kashmiri Chilli Powder (2 teaspoons)
  • Cumin Powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Coriander Powder (2 teaspoons)
  • Salt (1 teaspoon)
  • Garam Masala (1 teaspoon)
  • Butter
  • Fresh Cream (to garnish)
  • Coriander leaves (to garnish)

Recipe :

  1. Pressure cook the black gram and red kidney beans together.
  2. Pressure cook them for about 5 whistles and keep them aside.
  3. Grind the tomatoes and make a fine puree.
  4. Once done, mash the dal roughly.
  5. Heat some oil and butter in a pan, add onions and saute till they are brown.
  6. Add ginger garlic paste and saute along.
  7. Add the tomato puree and mix well.
  8. Close and cook for about 10 mins.
  9. Saute till the oil separates.
  10. Add the spice powders, and salt and saute.
  11. Add the cooked dals to the masala and add water and stir well.
  12. Add garam masala and mix well.
  13. Close and let it simmer for 10 mins.
  14. Turn off the stove and add a dollop of butter and mix well.
  15. Finally garnish the dal with fresh cream and coriander leaves.
