Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

    To marinate the Chicken :

  • 6 Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs (cut into tikka shapes)
  • Plain Yogurt (1 cup)
  • Juice of 1/2 Lemon
  • 3 finely grated cloves of Garlic
  • Finely grated Ginger (1 tablespoon)
  • Garam Masala (2 teaspoons)
  • Salt to taste (1/2 teaspoon)

    • For the Tikka masala sauce :

  • Vegetable Oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • 2 finely chopped Onion
  • 3 finely grated cloves of Garlic
  • Finely grated Ginger (1 tablespoon)
  • Turmeric Powder (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Ground Coriander (1 teaspoon)
  • Ground Cumin (1 teaspoon)
  • Garam Masala (1 teaspoon)
  • Red chilli Powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Tomato Puree (2 cups)
  • Heavy Cream (1 cup)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (to garnish)

Recipe

  1. In a large pot, heat oil on Medium-High. When hot, add chicken pieces, ensuring not to crowd the pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes a side, until browned. Set aside. The Chicken doesn't have to be cooked through at this stage
  2. In the same pan, add butter and Onions, and cook until soft and translucent. Add Ginger and Garlic, and sautee for 30-45 seconds. Then add Turmeric, Coriander Powder, Cumin, Garam Masala. Fry until fragrant (15-20 seconds).
  3. Add Tomato Sauce, Ground Red Chilli and season with salt to taste. Simmer for 15-20 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally until Tomato Sauce thickens, and takes on a deep reddish brown colour.
  4. Add Chicken and Heavy Cream, and cook for an additional 10 minutes until mixture is bubbling, glossy, and thick.
  5. Garnish with fresh Coriander and serve with hot Basmati Rice, and/or Naan/Rotis.
